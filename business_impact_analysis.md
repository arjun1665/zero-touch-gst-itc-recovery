## Executive Summary

Indian businesses collectively lose over **₹1.5 lakh crore annually** in unclaimed or delayed Input Tax Credit due to manual reconciliation gaps, vendor non-compliance, and filing errors. The Zero-Touch GST ITC Recovery platform eliminates these losses through an autonomous multi-agent AI system that operates end-to-end — from data ingestion to filing-ready output — with zero manual intervention.

This document quantifies the operational and financial impact for a representative **mid-market firm** (₹50–200 Cr annual turnover).
